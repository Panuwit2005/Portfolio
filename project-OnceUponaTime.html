Once Upon a Time

About the Project

"Once Upon a Time" is a story-driven horror game. You play as Jake, a frail young boy who must survive in a world of familiar fairy tales. Armed only with his notebook and the medicine his mother told him to always keep close, Jake must find a way to stay alive.

Project Info

  • Role : Gameplay Programmer
  • Engine : Unity / C#
  • Platform : PC (Windows)
  • Team Size : 3
