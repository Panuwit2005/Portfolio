Shadows of the Past

About the Project

"Shadows of the Past" is a story-based horror game where players take on the role of a police officer investigating an abandoned orphanage. Through haunting sounds and atmosphere, they slowly uncover repressed memories of their own childhood buried deep within.

Project Info

  • Role : Gameplay Programmer / Level Designer
  • Engine : Unity / C#
  • Platform : PC (Windows)
  • Team Size : 3
