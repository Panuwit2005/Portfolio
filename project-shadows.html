Shadows of the Past

About the Project

"Shadows of the Past" is a story-based horror game. You play as a police officer investigating an abandoned orphanage, slowly uncovering repressed memories of your own childhood as you go deeper into the house.

Project Info

  • Role : Gameplay Programmer / Level Designer
  • Engine : Unity / C#
  • Platform : PC (Windows)
  • Team Size : 3
